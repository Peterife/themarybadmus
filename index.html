About the Author

Mary Badmus is a writer, speaker, and storyteller whose soulful, practical voice inspires growth, clarity, and quiet strength. Through her books and heartfelt stories, she creates space—on page and stage—for people to reconnect with purpose and rise into their best selves. Author of three books (with a fourth on the way), her message is simple but powerful: you are capable, you are worthy, and your journey still matters.
