Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie

Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie was born in Enugu, Nigeria in 1977. She grew up on the campus of the University of Nigeria, Nsukka...

She has a Master’s Degree in Creative Writing from Johns Hopkins University and a Master of Arts degree in African Studies from Yale University. She currently lives between the United States and Nigeria.
